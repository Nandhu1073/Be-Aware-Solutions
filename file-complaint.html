How to File a Cybercrime Complaint

Follow these simple steps to report a cybercrime incident

1

Visit the Official Portal

Click the button below to open the National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal in a new tab.

Open Cybercrime Portal
2

Select Complaint Type

On the portal, you'll see two options:

  • Report Women/Child Related Crime
  • Report Other Cyber Crime

Choose the appropriate option based on your complaint.

3

Provide Personal Details

You'll need to enter:

  • Your full name
  • Valid mobile number
  • Email address
  • Address details
Make sure to provide accurate contact details as the police will use these to communicate with you.
4

Incident Details

Provide detailed information about the incident:

  • Date and time of the incident
  • Description of what happened
  • Any financial loss details
  • Relevant screenshots or documents
5

Submit and Track

After submission:

  • You'll receive a complaint number
  • Save this number for future reference
  • Use it to track your complaint status
Take a screenshot or note down your complaint number immediately after submission.

💻 Tech Support Scam

Identify attempts by fake IT agents pretending to fix your computer.

Common Signs:
  • 🛠️ Caller claims your device is infected or hacked
  • 📥 Asks you to install "support tools" (malware)
  • 🔐 Requests remote access or admin credentials

🧑‍💼 HR Impersonation

Learn how attackers impersonate HR personnel to trick employees.

Example Tactics:
  • 📄 Requests for sensitive personal data “to update records”
  • 🪪 Fakes urgency related to benefits, taxes, or payroll
  • 📧 Uses spoofed HR emails or fake forms
Play HR Pretexting Scenario

🖥️ IT Department Access Request

Detect suspicious requests for access to internal systems.

Warning Signs:
  • 🔒 "Routine maintenance" access requests from unknown staff
  • 🛑 No official communication or ticketing reference
  • 💬 Vague or inconsistent details about the issue
